What is Pranayama?

Pranayama is the yogic practice of conscious breath control. It uses specific patterns of inhalation, exhalation and breath retention to improve energy, focus and inner balance.

Top Benefits of Pranayama

1. Reduces Stress and Anxiety

Slow breathing activates the relaxation response and can help calm the nervous system after a stressful day.

2. Improves Lung Capacity

Regular breathing practice strengthens respiratory awareness and supports better oxygen intake.

3. Supports Better Sleep

Gentle pranayama before bedtime can calm mental activity and prepare the body for deeper rest.

4. Enhances Focus

Breath awareness improves concentration, clarity and emotional regulation during work and daily life.

5. Helps Weight Management

Practices like Kapalabhati can support digestion and metabolism when combined with yoga, food discipline and consistency.

Best Techniques for Beginners

  • Anulom Vilom for balance and calm
  • Bhramari for stress relief
  • Ujjayi for focus
  • Kapalabhati for energy
  • Sheetali for cooling the body
